Exercises - Linear Motion

  1. The speed of a nerve impulse in the human body is about 100 m/s. If you accidentally stud your toe in the dark, estimate the time it takes the nerve impulse to travel to your brain.
    Answer: The answer would depend on your height.
  2. Light travels at a speed of about 3 x 108 m/s. How many meters do a pulse of light travels in a time interval of 0.1 s?
    Answer: 3 x 107m
  3. A car accelerates uniformly from rest to a speed of 40.0 mi/h in 12.0 s. Find (a) the distance the car travels during this time and (b) the constant acceleration of the car.
    Answer: (a) 480m, (b) 33 m/s2
  4. A certain car is capable of accelerating at a rate of 0.60 m/s2. How long does it take for this car to go from a speed of 55 mi/h to a speed of 60 mi/h?
    Answer: 8.33s
