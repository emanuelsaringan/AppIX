Exercises - Free Fall Acceleration

  1. A ball is thrown directly downward with an initial speed of 8.00 m/s, from a height 30.0 m. After what time interval does it strike the ground?
    Answer: 0.091s
  2. A ball is thrown vertically upward with a speed of 25.0 m/s.
    1. How high does it rise?
    2. How long does it take to reach its highest point?
    3. How long does a ball take to hit the ground after it reaches its highest point?
    4. What is its velocity when it returns to the level from which it started?
    Answer: (a) 31.9m, (b) 2.55s
