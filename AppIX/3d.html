Exercises - Momentum and Impulse

  1. A boy of mass 35.0 kg jumps horizontally out of a stationary boat of a mass 70 kg. The boy jumps at a velocity of 3.00 m/s. At what velocity does the boat move back? Neglect any resistance due to the water.
    Answer: -1.5m/s
  2. Two cars travelling in opposite directions at 36.0 m/s collide head-on and lock together. One car has a mass of 1000 kg; the other has a mass of 800 kg. What is the speed after collision?
    Answer: 236m/s
